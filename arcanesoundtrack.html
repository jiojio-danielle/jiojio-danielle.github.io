Arcane's Soundtrack

Arcane has a multitude of songs sung by various artists, and musical scores that make you feel every moment in the show.

“Playground” Written by Mako and Sebastien Najand, sung by Bea Miller

“Our Love” Written by Curtis Harding, and Sam Cohen, performed by Curtis Harding and Jazmine Sullivan

“Goodbye” Written by Mako and sung by Ramsey

“Dirty Little Animals” Written by BONES UK, and Sebastien Najand, performed by BONES UK

“Enemy” Written and performed by Imagine Dragons and JID

“Guns for Hire” Written by Mako and performed by Woodkid

“Misfit toys” Written and performed by Pusha T and Mako

“Dynasties and Dystopia” Written and performed by Denzel Curry, Gizzle and Bren Joy

“Snakes” Written and performed by Pvris and Miyavi

“When Everything Went Wrong” Written and performed by Fantastic Negrito

“What Could Have Been” Written by Mako, performed by Sting and Ray Chen

Imagine Dragons and J.I.D make an appearance as an in show band and its awesome!
