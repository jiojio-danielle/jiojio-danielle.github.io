Arcane's Show Ratings

Arcane show ratings: The show has been praised for its success and brilliant storytelling, getting an 100% approval rating with an average rating of 9.10/10 on the review website Rotten Tomatoes. Ranking as Netflix’s number one show in 2021, ranked first on Netfilx’s Top 10 chart in 52 countries and set the record as Netflix’s highest rated series within the week of its premiere. Was nominated for many awards and won most of them such as the Annie Awards, The Game Awards – Best Adaptation, and Primetime Emmys Awards – Outstanding Animated Program and Outstanding Indivdual Achievemnet in Animation.
